Best Hamburger Ever

The best burger recipe for summertime grilling! This juicy burger is jam-packed with all kinds of stuff and has no tasteless bread crumbs. Serve on buns with your favorite condiments.

  • Prep Time
  • Cook Time
  • Total Time
  • Servings
  • 10 Mins
  • 10 Mins
  • 20 Mins
  • 5

Ingredients

  • 4 cups vegetable oil for frying
  • 5 small russet potatoes
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons chicken bouillon granules
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup milk

Directions

step 1

Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

step 2

Slice potatoes into 16 wedges.

step 3

Combine flour, chili powder, chicken bouillon, paprika, black pepper, and cayenne in a 1-gallon resealable plastic bag.

step 4

Dip potato wedges in milk, then shake them in the flour mixture. Fry in the hot oil until golden brown, 5 to 6 minutes per batch. Let drain on paper towels.
